About Us

At MISTraLab, we tackle complex challenges in materials discovery, design and optimization by integrating computational modeling, informatics and experimental data. Our mission is to accelerate scientific breakthroughs by developing cutting-edge tools and methodologies at the intersection of materials science and artificial intelligence.
